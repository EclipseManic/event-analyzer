EVTX investigation viewer
Turn Windows .evtx logs into a searchable investigation.
Event-Analyzer uploads EVTX files, parses them with a Rust backend, and stores events in a local SQLite index. Analysts review the details, while others get a clear, structured view and exportable results.
Core problem
Windows EVTX logs are raw, noisy, and spread across many files — difficult to read quickly or explain to others.
How it solves it
Event-Analyzer parses EVTX files into a searchable, filterable workspace and keeps everything under one investigation.
Who it helps
Analysts investigate details, freshers follow the guided flow, and leaders review outputs via exports.
